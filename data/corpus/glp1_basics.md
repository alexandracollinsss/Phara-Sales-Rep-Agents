## Indication Summary (US — verify current labeling)

- **Mounjaro (tirzepatide):** Adjunct to diet and exercise to improve glycemic control in adults with type 2 diabetes mellitus.
- **Zepbound (tirzepatide):** Chronic weight management in adults with obesity (BMI ≥30) or overweight (BMI ≥27) with at least one weight-related comorbidity.
- **Ozempic (semaglutide):** Type 2 diabetes; CV risk reduction in established CVD per label.
- **Wegovy (semaglutide 2.4 mg):** Chronic weight management; SELECT supports CV benefit in overweight/obesity with CVD without diabetes.

## Evidence Hierarchy for Physician AI

When answering comparative questions, prioritize: (1) head-to-head RCTs in matching population (e.g., SURPASS-2, SURMOUNT-4); (2) pivotal placebo-controlled trials (SURMOUNT-1, SELECT); (3) indirect comparisons only when direct evidence absent.

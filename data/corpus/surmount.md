# SURMOUNT Program — Tirzepatide in Obesity / Overweight (Eli Lilly)

## SURMOUNT-1: Obesity Without Diabetes (NEJM 2022)

**Citation:** Jastreboff AM, Aronne LJ, Ahmad NN, et al. Tirzepatide once weekly for the treatment of obesity. *N Engl J Med*. 2022;387(3):205-216. **PMID:** [35658024](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/35658024/)

**Design:** Phase 3, 2,539 adults (BMI ≥30, or ≥27 with weight-related comorbidity except diabetes); 72-week treatment.

**Interventions:** Tirzepatide 5, 10, or 15 mg vs placebo, all with lifestyle intervention.

**Primary endpoint:** Percent change in body weight from baseline at 72 weeks.

**Key results:**
- Mean percent weight change: placebo −3.1%; tirzepatide 5 mg −15.0%; 10 mg −19.5%; **15 mg −20.9%**.
- ≥5% weight loss: 89–96% with tirzepatide vs 28% placebo; ≥15% weight loss: 56–63% with 10–15 mg vs 13% placebo.
- Improvements in cardiometabolic measures vs placebo.

**Regulatory:** Supported FDA approval of **Zepbound** (tirzepatide) for chronic weight management.

## SURMOUNT-2: Overweight / Obesity With Type 2 Diabetes (Lancet 2023)

**Citation:** Garvey WT, Birkenfeld AL, Dicker D, et al. Two-year effects of tirzepatide in adults with obesity or overweight with type 2 diabetes (SURMOUNT-2). *Lancet*. 2023;402(10402):753-765. **PMID:** [37541289](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/37541289/)

**Design:** 938 adults with BMI ≥27 and T2DM (HbA1c 7–10% on diet/exercise ± metformin).

**Key results at 72 weeks:**
- Mean weight change: tirzepatide 10 mg −12.8%, 15 mg −14.7% vs placebo −3.2%.
- Substantial A1c reductions alongside weight loss.

**Clinical takeaway:** Tirzepatide addresses both weight and glycemic control in patients with obesity and T2DM.

## SURMOUNT-4: Tirzepatide vs Semaglutide 2.4 mg (JAMA Intern Med 2024)

**Citation:** Aronne LJ, Ahmad NN, Auerbach P, et al. Comparative efficacy of tirzepatide and semaglutide on body weight in adults with obesity (SURMOUNT-4). *JAMA Intern Med*. 2024;184(9):1053-1060. **PMID:** [39068590](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/39068590/)

**Design:** Phase 3b open-label; adults with obesity (BMI ≥30 or ≥27 with comorbidity); 84-week total (36-week lead-in on semaglutide 2.4 mg, then randomized).

**Key results (switch period):** After 36 weeks on semaglutide 2.4 mg, patients switched to tirzepatide achieved **additional mean weight loss** vs continued semaglutide; tirzepatide superior for percent weight change and proportion achieving ≥5% additional loss.

**Clinical takeaway:** Direct comparative evidence in chronic weight management; relevant when physicians compare **Zepbound/Mounjaro (tirzepatide)** vs **Wegovy (semaglutide 2.4 mg)**.
